Real-Time Semantic Video Communication with Temporally Consistent Diffusion Models

1 Chair of Media Technology, 2 Chair of Communication Networks, 3 Munich Institute of Robotics and Machine Intelligence
School of Computation, Information and Technology

Summary

This paper introduces CVSC, a real-time and temporally consistent semantic video communication approach. This website presents visual results generated with our approach and the baselines. We utilize the semantic video coding approach introduced previously [1]. The previous approach utilized OASIS for synthesis. Our method utilizes diffusion models for video synthesis. Our novel methodology enables synthesis in as few as four diffusion steps. Therefore, we additionally compared ControlVideo and ControlVideo-4Step [2]. The latter is ControlVideo, but we synthesize the videos in 4 diffusion steps.
